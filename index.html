Summer Rice Salad

Ingredients

  • Small shallot, minced
  • White wine vinegar
  • Loosely packed lime zest
  • Fresh lime juice
  • Dijon mustard
  • Honey
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Cooked basmati rice, chilled
  • Thinly sliced napa cabbage
  • Thinly sliced English cucmbers
  • Diced red bell pepper
  • Thinly sliced green onions
  • Chopped fresh mint
  • Chopped fresh chives
  • Chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • Salt to taste

How to Prepare

  • Stir together shallot, vinegar, lime zest, lime juice, Dijon mustard, and honey in a large bowl
  • Whisk in olive oil until blended
  • Add rice, cabbage, cucmbers, red bell pepper, green onions, mint, chives and parsley; toss.
  • Add salt to taste.
  • Cover and chill up to 4 hours
